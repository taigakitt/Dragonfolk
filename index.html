World Lore

Abyssal Maw

Central Chasm · Sacred Site

The Abyssal Maw

At the heart of the circular continent, Goryūto, all rivers converge and crash in endless waterfalls into a mist-and-myt shrouded chasm no folk has ever reached the bottom of.

Ringing Plains

Central · Fertile Fields

The Ringing Plains

Lush, fertile soil surrounding the Maw. Trade is abundant.

Great Dragon Spine

Northeast · Mountain Range

The Great Dragon Spine

Jagged, snow-capped peaks threaded with ancient ley lines. The mountains channel raw magical energy through the earth — the ruling Houses built in their shadow. Koryuu-gyeong, the capital, nests at the base. Onkei-shuu, a province that neighbors the capital and goverend by Saiwa, Akagi, and Ryuhwa Houses.

Mistwood Expanse

Southeast · Enchanted Forest

Mistwood Expanse

Ancient trees and shifting magical paths. Bioluminescent undergrowth and rarities.

Driftcrag Isles

Coastal · Archipelago

The Driftcrag Isles

Rugged cliffs and scattered islands where piracy and vital maritime trade clash. The Isles have no lord — only captains.

Ashari Wastes

West · Desert

The Ashari Wastes

Barren deserts and the ruins of civilizations.

Upper Caste
Horned Ones

Revered, with minimal scales and great magical aptitude. They bear horns and tails.

Middle Caste
Scaled Ones

Fully scaled, sturdy and non-magical. Humanoid in face and form. Often builders, soldiers, and craftsfolk.

Lower Caste
Wyrm Kin

Marginalized, dismissed. Digitigrade legs and more bestial in form. The slur "wyrm" originates here.

Dieties
Old Ones

Ancient dragons of old, tied to religious folk belief. Worshipped as divine progenitors to Dragonfolk.

Annual Event
The Saegu Matsuri

A festival that celebrates the turn into a new year. The past is remembered, the new is encouraged.
